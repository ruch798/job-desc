Part-Time Business Development Executive

Outbound and Partnerships

We are looking for a part-time Business Development Executive to help us secure brand partnerships and media placements through outbound outreach.

Your role is to start conversations, open doors, and book opportunities. You will not be negotiating or closing deals. Your responsibility is outbound reachout and lead generation only.

Brand Outreach

  • Reach out to brands and companies via LinkedIn and email
  • Pitch collaboration, sponsorship, and partnership opportunities
  • Identify marketing, growth, and partnerships decision-makers
  • Book qualified intro calls

Media and Agency Outreach

  • Reach out to PR agencies, media agencies, editors, and publications
  • Pitch founder stories, expert articles, interviews, and podcast features
  • Secure media placement opportunities and introductions

Requirements

  • Strong written communication skills
  • Comfortable with cold outreach
  • Basic understanding of sales, partnerships, or lead generation
  • Self-motivated and consistent
  • Ability to work independently

Compensation

  • Commission-based
  • 10 to 20 percent of first deal value for brand partnerships
  • Fixed payout per successful media placement
  • Top performers may be offered a full-time role

Application Process (Required)
